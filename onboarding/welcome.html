Welcome to HumanStamp

HumanStamp lets you sign content you personally typed. You can optionally add your name to signatures, or leave it blank to use ✍ HumanStamped.

Before you start: close and reopen any open Gmail or Outlook tabs (or refresh them) so HumanStamp can load in your compose window.
